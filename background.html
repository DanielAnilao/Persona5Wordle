Persodle R

A Persona based Wordle.
Inspired by all of the other wordle-like spin-offs.

Rules:

Match the Arcana, Inherit Type and Elemental Affinities of the Persona of the Day.
After submitting a Persona, you will be told if you match the Arcana and/or the Inherit Type or not.
In terms of the Elemental Affinities you will get:
A green if you match the element.
An orange if the element is affiliated with the Persona.
A red if the Persona is neutral to the element.
